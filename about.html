About the AI Research Lab

CIS Department, Faculty of Science & Information Technology, Daffodil International University

CIS Department

The Department of Computing and Information System (CIS) is part of the Faculty of Science and Information Technology at Daffodil International University — one of Bangladesh's top-ranked universities for research and innovation.

UGC Approved FSIT Faculty Industry-linked
Background

Excellence Through Collaboration

The Department of Computing and Information System (CIS) at Daffodil International University has been continuously fostering academic excellence through industry-academia collaboration — especially by integrating industry demands of ICT skills into the core curriculum of the CIS program.

The AI Research Lab is the department's dedicated research arm, bringing together faculty members, visiting researchers, and students to work on cutting-edge problems in Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, and Data Science.

DIU is ranked 2nd among all universities and 1st among all private universities in Bangladesh based on SCOPUS-indexed research publications (2022). The university also holds a QS Asia University Ranking of 280th (2025) and is ranked 484th globally in QS Sustainability Rankings 2026.

Vision

To be recognized as a leading AI and computing research hub in South Asia, producing graduates and research that shape industry and society.

Goals

Conduct peer-reviewed research, mentor researchers at all levels, and build partnerships with technology and social-impact organizations.

Research Areas

Six Domains of Focus

The AI Research Lab works across six interconnected AI disciplines, each addressing real-world challenges faced by Bangladesh and the global community.

Machine Learning

Classical and modern ML algorithms — regression, decision trees, ensemble methods, and reinforcement learning — applied to real-world prediction and classification problems.

Deep Learning

Design and training of neural architectures including CNNs, RNNs, Transformers, and Generative models. Focuses on large-scale training and efficient inference.

Computer Vision

Image recognition, object detection, semantic segmentation, and video understanding — with applications in medical imaging, agriculture, and public safety.

Natural Language Processing

Language modeling, sentiment analysis, machine translation, and NLP tools specifically tailored for the Bangla language — serving over 230 million speakers worldwide.

Robotics

Autonomous navigation, robotic manipulation, multi-agent coordination, and human-robot interaction aimed at deployable real-world systems.

Data Science

Large-scale data analysis, statistical modeling, time-series forecasting, and visualization for evidence-driven decision making across industries and government.

Contributions

Academic & Industry Impact

Academic Research

DIU is ranked #1 among private universities in Bangladesh by SCOPUS-indexed publications. The CIS department's faculty and student researchers regularly publish in top IEEE, ACM, Springer, and Elsevier venues including ICECE, STI, COMPAS, and more.

Industry Collaboration

The department maintains active collaboration with global partners including Microsoft, Google, AWS, IEEE, and WHO. CIS graduates are employed across leading tech companies in Bangladesh and internationally, validating the department's industry-integrated curriculum.

Specialized Labs

DIU supports dedicated research infrastructure including a Cyber Security Lab, NLP & Machine Learning Research Lab, and an Industrial Innovation Centre — giving researchers access to cutting-edge tools and infrastructure.

Student Development

Life at Daffodil is eventful and skill-focused. Students grow beyond the classroom through meaningful research opportunities, 60+ active clubs, workshops, and mentorship from experienced faculty — many of whom are active researchers with IEEE publications.
