Who We Are

Computing & Information System at DIU

The Department of Computing and Information System (CIS) has been continuously fostering academic excellence through industry-academia collaboration, integrating industry demands of ICT skills into its core curriculum. The AI Research Lab is the department's dedicated research wing.

Rigorous Research

The lab conducts peer-reviewed research in Machine Learning, Deep Learning, Computer Vision, NLP, Robotics, and Data Science — contributing to IEEE, ACM, and Springer publications.

Industry-Academia Bridge

DIU is ranked 2nd among all Bangladeshi universities in SCOPUS-indexed publications. The CIS department actively partners with industry to translate research into deployable solutions.

Student-Centric Culture

Students get hands-on experience through research internships, thesis supervision, lab projects, and collaboration with faculty on live research problems.

Research Focus

Six Core Domains

01
Machine Learning

Algorithms for prediction, classification, and decision-making applied to real-world datasets.

02
Deep Learning

Neural architecture design — CNNs, RNNs, Transformers, and GANs for large-scale tasks.

03
Computer Vision

Object detection, image segmentation, and visual scene understanding for medical and industrial use.

04
NLP

Language modeling, machine translation, and Bangla language AI research serving 230M+ speakers.

05
Robotics

Autonomous navigation, manipulation, and human-robot interaction for real-world deployment.

06
Data Science

Statistical modeling, large-scale data analysis, and visualization for evidence-based decision making.
